ARTIST - MAXIME
GALLAND

Maxime Galland is the official artist/game developer of Pandonia and owner of Alchemist Pictures; specializing in 3D art, animation, and game development. Maxime has showcased his talent and skills in a variety of ways and most notably has worked with Nintendo and Epic Games, he has also been hand-picked as a certified Unreal Engine 5 specialist. In addition, his team members have worked on Transformers, Batman: Arkham Knight, and the order. His animation team has also done work for BMW, Jaguar, Honda, Kinder, and other major companies.

FAQ

What is Pandonia?
Pandonia is a AA Battle Royale Game with a unique hand-to-hand combat system and NFT Integration. 10,000 original 4K crocs with high-quality 3D Art Rendered in Unreal Engine 5 by Maxime Galland.
How can I Mint/Purchase a Pandonia NFT?
In order to mint for Pandonia you will need an amazon account, to begin with. Next, you will go to amazon.com and select the digital assets page click on Pandonia and then click the purchase button.
Which platforms will pandonia be on?
Pandonia will be available to play on PC!
When will it be released?
The game is currently in production and is estimated to be completed within 9-12 months of mint.
